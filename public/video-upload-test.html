API Endpoints:

POST   /api/videos/upload     - Upload a video
GET    /api/videos            - List all company videos
GET    /api/videos/:id        - Get video details
PUT    /api/videos/:id        - Update video metadata
DELETE /api/videos/:id        - Delete a video

Getting Started:

  1. Login: POST /api/auth/login
  2. Select Company: POST /api/auth/select-company
  3. Copy the accessToken from the response
  4. Paste it in the "Access Token" field above
  5. Click "Save Token & Load Videos"
  6. Upload videos and manage them!
